About Amanda

Amanda Van Huis is a website designer/developer from Detroit, MI.

Amanda has been designing websites since 2003. Her first website was a George Harrison appreciation page.

Amanda attended Wayne State University from 2008 to 2013 with a Bachelor's in Public Affairs. Amanda went on to work in the nonprofit sector for two years before deciding to pursue her passion for web design and development by taking a Front-End Web Development course at Grand Circus in downtown Detroit.

Amanda is looking forward to taking her knowledge to the next level!
